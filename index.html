WHO AM I

My name is Marry, and I work at a school with children. Teaching has always been more than just a job for me — it is my true passion. I love spending time with kids, helping them discover new things, and watching their progress every day.

Working with children inspires me because every lesson brings joy, challenges, and smiles. I believe that education is not only about giving knowledge but also about supporting young minds, encouraging curiosity, and helping them believe in themselves.

I enjoy creating lessons that are interesting and engaging, using different activities so that every student can feel involved and motivated.
My greatest reward is seeing the happiness in a child’s eyes when they learn something new or succeed in something they thought was difficult.
For me, being a teacher is not only about teaching—it is about guiding, inspiring, and making a positive difference in the lives of children. I am proud to be part of their journey and to help them grow into confident, kind, and capable people. перевод
